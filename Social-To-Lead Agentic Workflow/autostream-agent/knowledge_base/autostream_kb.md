# AutoStream – Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators.
It uses AI to help YouTubers, Instagram creators, and other social media professionals produce
high-quality videos faster, without needing professional editing skills.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Videos:** Up to 10 videos per month
- **Resolution:** Up to 720p
- **Support:** Email support (business hours)
- **AI Captions:** Not included
- **Best for:** Hobbyists and new creators getting started

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Videos:** Unlimited videos per month
- **Resolution:** Up to 4K resolution
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support
- **AI Captions:** Included
- **Best for:** Full-time content creators and growing channels

## Features Overview

### Automated Editing
- Smart cut detection
- Auto color correction
- Background noise removal
- Intro/outro templates

### AI-Powered Tools
- AI captions (Pro only)
- Auto-highlight reel generation
- Smart thumbnail suggestions
- Voice-to-text transcription

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Free trial available for 7 days
- **No refunds after the 7-day trial period**
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle

### Support Policy
- **Basic Plan:** Email support during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST)
- **Pro Plan:** 24/7 live chat and priority email support

### Billing
- Monthly and annual billing available
- Annual billing offers 2 months free (equivalent to ~17% discount)
- All major credit cards accepted; PayPal available

## FAQ

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades at next billing cycle.

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes, all new users get a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan.

**What video formats are supported?**
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM for input and output.

**Can I use AutoStream for a team?**
Team plans are coming soon. Currently, accounts are individual.
